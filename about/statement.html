virtually conflicted reality

I — Lark VCR — work in video, installation, critical design, and performance. VCR stands for Virtually Conflicted Reality: A common thread in my work is multiplicity of meaning and experience in an increasingly digitized world. Many of my projects explore the personal, political, and social implications of the dissolving boundary between body and machine. I use humor within a critical queer framework to tackle subject matter as diverse as data privacy, sexual violence culture, compulsory engagement with social media, and human-computer desire.
