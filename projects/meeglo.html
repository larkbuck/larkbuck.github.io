Meeglo

Startup Company

Supported by the CITRIS Foundry Tech Incubator

learn more about the Stake in the Heart Series

Meeglo booth at Berkeley Innovation Fair 2015

Meeglo was accepted into the 2015 cohort of the CITRIS Foundry Incubator. The startup was a genuine attempt to create a tool for deep breathing, specifically for people with activated nervous systems.

Though not necessarily an "Art" piece in itself, Meeglo was the catalyst for the Stake In the Heart series, seeding distrust for technologies of biosurveillance and startup culture at large.
