digital video, 17min, 2016


Tattle-Tale Heart is a speculative fiction about privacy and connection in social media. Characters dance on the double-edged sword of technological innovation as they navigate seductive technologies of biosurveillence in this dark/light-hearted portrait of queer subculture.
