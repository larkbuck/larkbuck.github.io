seeding speculative futures

As a personals ads website, human-computer love invites you to participate in imagining future technologies that you want to see (or love, or fuck).

Speculative fiction is an invaluable tool for reflecting on our current cultural moment, while also planting seeds to affect future technologies, politics, and desire. We are at an opportune time to affect the future of human-computer intimacy. While the technology is rapidly advancing, industries for virtual/machine sex are not yet fully institutionalized or legislated, and public opinion is only beginning to take shape.

Now is a fertile time to apply our imagination towards radical visions for technological intimacy, so that we might explore potential beyond those shaped by capitalist forces that reinforce the status quo.
