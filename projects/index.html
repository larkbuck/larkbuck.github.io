Tattletale Heart, 17min, 2016

Short film exploring erosion of privacy under the seduction of social media. As much as it is a dystopic fiction, the film is also a campy portrait of queer subculture.

Heart Startups

Learn more about the Heart Startups, a project series exploring biosurveillance, data privacy, and the trappings of social media. Multiple stages of world-building include development of three heart monitors branded as startups, which then infiltrated tech spaces and culminated in the short film, Tatttletale Heart.

Health Data for Employers

Discreetly posed as a business card holder, Babump (“buh-bump”) picks up signals from compatible heart monitors and displays health data on the back of the device. With Babump, you can track your employees’ cardiovascular system in real time.
